# Points and Lounge Strategy: The World of Hyatt & Bilt Rewards Playbook

This consolidated guide details the strategy for maximizing World of Hyatt (WoH) points—transferred primarily from Bilt Rewards—for aspirational travel and high-value redemptions.

---

## 1. High-Value Redemption Strategy (Hyatt Transfers)

The optimal strategy for Bilt Rewards is a 1:1 transfer to World of Hyatt, which often yields a redemption value of 2+ cents per point (CPP), nearly doubling the value compared to standard travel portals (1.25 CPP).

## 3. VIP Perks & Credit Burn Strategy
